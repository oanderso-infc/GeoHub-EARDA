GeoHub EARDA

This site hosts openly accessible Environmental Assessment and Reporting Data Area (EARDA) data products for spatial analysis and planning. Browse categorized datasets and access project-specific documentation.

Census Data

Census Data includes population, socioeconomic, and demographic variables sourced from the latest national census. Use these datasets for community profiles, deprivation studies, and more.
Census Blocks (2021)
Vector boundaries and key indicators for all census blocks in the study area. Includes population, age, and household characteristics.

Infrastructure Data

Infrastructure Data features spatial layers for public assets, utilities, and critical infrastructure networks. Useful for resilience assessment, capital planning, and service area analysis.
Public Facilities Inventory
Comprehensive spatial database of public buildings, health, and educational facilities for the region.

Climate Data

Climate Data includes open geospatial datasets on precipitation, temperature, flood risk, and other environmental hazards. Suitable for climate risk mapping and adaptation research.
Flood Susceptibility Index
Raster-based index of flood susceptibility for urban and peri-urban areas. Useful for risk assessments and policy support.

Transportation Data

Transportation Data features datasets for transit lines, stations, accessibility metrics, and more. Analyze mobility, connectivity, and equity across the network.
SAM Score Approximation Project
Station Accessibility Measure (SAM) scores and visualizations for rapid transit stations.
Multi-Modal Network Example
Sample dataset for integrated multi-modal transit networks, including bus, subway, and cycling connections.
Transit Ridership Trends
Data visualizing annual and monthly ridership for key transit lines and stations from 2010-2023.
Active Transportation Paths
Sample layer of cycling lanes, multi-use trails, and pedestrian pathways for assessing non-motorized mobility.
Accessibility Index by Station
Example accessibility index for subway and commuter rail stations, highlighting essential services proximity.
