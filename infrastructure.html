Infrastructure Data

Explore infrastructure datasets including public facilities, utilities, and transportation assets.

Public Facilities Inventory
Map and list of all public buildings, community centers, and recreation facilities in the region.
Utilities Network
Geospatial layers of electrical grids, water mains, and telecommunications lines.
Major Roadways
Dataset of major arterial roads, highways, and expressways, suitable for route and traffic analysis.
Bridges Database
Locations and metadata of bridges, including structural type and inspection status.
Sewer Networks
Mapping of major sewer and drainage lines for urban areas.
